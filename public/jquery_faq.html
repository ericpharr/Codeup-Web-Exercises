Facts about Big Bend National Park

  • On July 1, 1944 Big Bend National Park opens for the first time.
  • Over 100 species of plants are found here.
  • More than 450 species of birds have been recorded here, and 75 species of mammals have been observed.
  • The highest temperature was 114 degrees in 1982, and the record for the lowest temperature was 10 degrees in 1989.

Facts about Redwood National Park

  • Redwood National Park is the home for many wild lions, bears and elk.
  • The Endor scenes for Star Wars Episode 6: Return of the Jedi were filmed there.
  • Redwood trees can reach up to 300 feet or higher.
  • The amount of carbon in the upper two meters of soil alone is 14 million metric tons.

Facts about the Grand Canyon

  • The Grand Canyon is found in the US state of Arizona.
  • Many people consider it to be one of the seven wonders of the natural world.
  • The Grand Canyon is 277 miles (446 kilometres) in length.
  • At its widest point the Grand Canyon stretches 18 miles (29 kilometres) across.
